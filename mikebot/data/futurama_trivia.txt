Futurama premiered on Fox on March 28, 1999, created by Matt Groening and David X. Cohen.
The show was cancelled by Fox in 2003 but revived by Comedy Central in 2008 after strong DVD sales.
Fry was frozen on December 31, 1999 and woke up exactly 1000 years later on December 31, 2999.
John DiMaggio voices Bender and initially held out during the Hulu revival over pay disputes.
The Planet Express ship was designed to look like a headless chicken from certain angles.
Zoidberg is a doctor despite knowing nothing about human medicine - he is an expert on Decapodians.
The show contains dozens of real math and science jokes written by the writers, many of whom have PhDs.
Bender's full name is Bender Bending Rodriguez.
Nibbler's shadow appears in the pilot episode foreshadowing his role in Fry's freezing - a retcon added later.
The episode Jurassic Bark is considered one of the saddest episodes in animated TV history.
Kif Kroker is Lieutenant to Zapp Brannigan and eventually becomes the father of Amy's children.
The Omicronians Lrrr and Ndnd are obsessed with Earth television, especially Single Female Lawyer.
Hermes Conrad is a Grade 36 bureaucrat and former Olympic limbo champion.
The Professor's catchphrase is Good news, everyone! - though the news is rarely good.
Leela was raised believing she was an alien but is actually a mutant from the sewers of New York.
The show features a recurring gag of alien language codes that fans decoded into actual messages.
Billy West voices Fry, Professor Farnsworth, Zoidberg, and Zapp Brannigan - four main characters.
Futurama was revived again on Hulu in 2023 with most of the original cast returning.
The Robot Devil appears frequently and is a skilled violinist who strikes deals with characters.
Zapp Brannigan wears a velour uniform and is a parody of Captain Kirk from Star Trek.
The city of New New York is built on top of the ruins of Old New York.
Mom, the villain, runs MomCorp and secretly controls most robots on Earth.
The episode The Luck of the Fryrish features a twist ending that makes it one of the most emotional episodes.
Calculon is a famous robot actor whose melodramatic performances are a running gag.
Amy Wong is a rich Mars ranching heiress who interns at Planet Express.
The show popularized the phrase I, for one, welcome our new robot overlords.
Scruffy the janitor is a recurring background character who reads adult magazines and says little.
The Hypnotoad is a recurring gag - a toad that hypnotizes everyone, with its own TV show All My Circuits.
Futurama holds the Guinness World Record for most guest stars in an animated series.
The number 1729 appears throughout the series - it is the Hardy-Ramanujan taxicab number, a math joke.
Flexo is a bending unit who looks identical to Bender but has a goatee - a parody of evil twin tropes.
The episode Where No Fan Has Gone Before features all surviving original Star Trek cast members.
Fry has no delta brainwave due to being his own grandfather, making him immune to certain mind control.
The show features a recurring trio called the Why Not Zoidberg? meme before internet memes existed.
Leela's purple hair and single eye make her one of the most recognizable characters in animation.
The Planet Express crew regularly dies and is resurrected, treating death as a minor inconvenience.
Al Gore, Stephen Hawking, and other celebrities appeared as themselves in the show.
Bender claims to be 40% zinc and bends girders for a living before joining Planet Express.
The show's universe includes multiple Earths, time travel paradoxes, and parallel dimensions.
The finale Meanwhile involves Fry and Leela finally getting together after years of will-they-won't-they.
Morbo the news anchor is an alien who openly hates humans but continues working in Earth broadcasting.
The episode Roswell That Ends Well is set in 1947 Roswell and won the show's first Emmy Award.
The theme song was composed by Christopher Tyng, inspired by Edvard Grieg's In the Hall of the Mountain King.
Richard Nixon's head is preserved in a jar and serves as President of Earth throughout the series.
The Simpsons connection: Futurama is set in the same universe, with Bart Simpson's skeleton seen in one episode.
The show satirizes corporate greed, bureaucracy, environmentalism, and religion throughout its run.
Turanga Leela was named after the Turangalila-Symphonie by Olivier Messiaen.
Kif has no skeleton and can squeeze through any opening, which is played for comedy repeatedly.
The episode Time Keeps on Slipping features the Harlem Globetrotters as math professors.
Futurama's writers included Ken Keeler, who holds a PhD in mathematics from Harvard University.
